Preliminaries

Nairobi is a vibrant city undergoing rapid urban development. However, the growth has been accompanied by challenges, particularly concerning the status of buildings and tenant rights.

What We Do

The primary objective of the E-constructor platform is to enhance transparency in the construction and tenant management processes by democratizing building data and protecting both residents and stakeholders in the real estate sector.

Benefits to City Residents

  • Access to information: Residents can easily access real-time information on the status of buildings, including those under construction.
  • Prevention of document forgery: The platform provides an official channel for verifying court documents such as injunction notices.
  • Digital record-keeping: Secure digital records of eviction notices prevent backdating and ensure accountability.
  • Increased transparency: Helps build trust among residents, landlords, and government entities.
  • Cost-effective solutions: The USSD platform ensures inclusivity even for residents without smartphones.

Solution

The E-constructor USSD-coded platform is an innovative response to the building and tenancy challenges faced by residents of Nairobi and Kenya at large. It enhances transparency, prevents fraud, and protects citizens' rights.
